Nature

There is no greater beauty, inspiration or proof of Divine grace, than nature. From the ever changing fresco in the sky, to the most vile bottom feeder, natue's beauty multifaceted. It is our responsibility to use the ingenuity we have been graced with, to take care of nature.

Music

Everyone has some creative pursuit. Mine is music. Harmony and rhythm is what animates my spirit. I also am familiar with multiple instruments.

Flute

Portable, ancient in design and ethereal. Emulating the sweet song of the songbird and the mournful howls of the wind.

Piano

With 88 tones at my fingertips, I enjoy piecing together complex harmonies.

Cooking

Food is never really "good" unless you do it yourself. The cutting board is my palette, your plate is my canvas. I use the recipes I grew up with and continue to add to my collection. I am quite fond of Italian and Vietnamese cuisine.
